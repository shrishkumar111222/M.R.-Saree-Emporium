Our Story

Six Decades of Quality, Range & Relationships

What began in Surat's textile trade over sixty years ago is today a five-city wholesale house serving generations of families and thousands of retail businesses. The principles haven't changed: honest pricing, dependable quality, and a range wide enough that every shelf finds something that sells.

From everyday cotton sarees to bridal silk, from kurtis to home furnishing — every product is selected to the same standard, because when our partners grow, we grow.

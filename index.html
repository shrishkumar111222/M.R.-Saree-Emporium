Our Story

Decades of Trust in India's Textile Trade

From the heart of Kohinoor Textile Market on Surat's Ring Road, M.R. Saree Emporium has grown into a destination where merchants, retailers, resellers and families find everything under one roof — from everyday cotton sarees to bridal couture and home textiles.

Every bolt of fabric and every saree in our showroom reflects the same principle: honest pricing, dependable quality, and range that keeps your shelves fresh season after season.
